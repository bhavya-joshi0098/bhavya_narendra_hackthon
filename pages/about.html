About Welfare Schemes Portal

The Welfare Schemes Portal is designed to provide information and facilitate access to various welfare schemes offered by the Ahmedabad government municipal corporation. Our goal is to ensure that citizens can easily find and apply for the assistance they need.

We aim to empower individuals and families by providing them with the necessary resources and support through our comprehensive range of welfare schemes.
